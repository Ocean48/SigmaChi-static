    About Us
    The Kappa Mu Chapter of Sigma Chi was founded on April 9th, 1994. At the time it is the 228th chapter and ninth in Canada.

    Located at the University of Windsor, in Windsor, Ontario, the Kappa Mu Chapter is the preeminent collegiate leadership development organization on its campus.

    The chapter is active in both terms of the year. It holds numerous philanthropic, fundraising and social events in and around the Windsor area.

    To read more about the chapter's news and find out about recent events visit the Kappa Mu Facebook page and Instagram.

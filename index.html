The Kappa Mu Chapter of Sigma Chi was founded on April 9th, 1994. At the time of its founding, it became Sigma Chi's 228th chapter and Canada's ninth chapter.

Located at the University of Windsor, the Kappa Mu Chapter is the preeminent collegiate leadership development organization on its campus.

The chapter is active both terms of the year: Fall and Winter. It holds numerous social, philanthropic and leadership building events in and around the Windsor area.

Founded in 1855, Sigma Chi is one of the oldest and largest college fraternities with 237 chapters throughout North America. The fundamental purpose of the Sigma Chi Fraternity is the cultivation, maintenance and accomplishment of the ideals of friendship, justice, and learning within our membership.

Recruitment


What is Rush?

University students interested in Greek Life typically go through a process called Rush. Rush is a series of social events organized by the fraternity throughout the first month of the school year. Certain rush events involve only the chapter, such as our Pasta Information night, whereas others involve all students living in residence, such as our campus-wide Midnight Capture the Flag event. All rush events are paid for by the fraternity. The purpose of rush is for potential new brothers to meet the active chapter.

Why Rush a Fraternity?

Joining a fraternity provides limitless benefits:

  • For students going into their first year it is an excellent way to meet new people. New members will have the opportunity to go through the rush process with students just like them.
  • A student entering into his first year of engineering can meet brothers who are in their fourth year of engineering and are willing to provide notes, textbooks and tips for academic achievements.
  • Being involved in the Fraternity provides the opportunity for many extracurricular activities. Sigma Chi works year-round to organize philanthropic events such as our fundraiser and Heaven & Hell Halloween Party.
  • Each fraternity has its own array of alumni (graduated brothers) who strive to help their undergraduate brothers succeed by providing career referrals and business connections.
  • By simply being part of the Fraternity, students have the ability to apply for and receive scholarships & bursaries that are exclusive to members of Sigma Chi.
  • Becoming a brother of Sigma Chi at any chapter (University) means that the brother is part of all 244 active chapters across North America. Brothers from different chapters will often visit one another for social events, initiations, and philanthropic fundraisers.

How do I rush a Fraternity?

Each potential new member, or ‘Rush’, must attend at least two rush events in order to be considered eligible for pledgeship (the next process to joining any Fraternity). The full rush schedule will be advertised on social media for everyone to see. Each rush event will begin by meeting at our chapter house on 3058 Peter Street at the designated time and date.

FAQ's


 

Do I need to be in my first year in order to rush?

No. A student in any year of study at the University of Windsor may choose to rush Sigma Chi.

 

 

Can I bring my friends to rush events even if they do not want to rush?

Yes. Our rush events are more fun with a higher a turnout of people.

 

 

If I choose not to rush in the Fall semester, can I return in the Winter?

Yes. We understand and respect that some students may need more time to adjust to their new lives at university. Our winter rush begins during the Winter semester which is typically in January.
